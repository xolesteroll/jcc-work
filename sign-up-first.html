In order to obtain a Qualified Certificate for Electronic Signature, you must apply through a Local Registration Authority of your choice.

The Local Registration Authority will provide the first-level approval of your application and is the place you need to visit following the completion of your application online.

Please select the Local Registration Authority through which you would like to apply for your certificate and proceed with the identification process:
